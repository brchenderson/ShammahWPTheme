Our Mission

We believe that every youth should have a clear Vision, Image and Purpose in life. Our mission is to empower the lives of young men and women who are in marginalized communities through educational and resource services.

Our Values

  • We believe that every young man and woman should be given a choice and an opportunity to succeed.
  • We believe having clear vision, image and purpose are the keys to success.
  • We believe that education, tools and relationships are the keys to empowerment.
  • We believe that the right resources are essential for expanding opportunity and growth.
  • We believe in advocating for the fatherless.
  • We believe in supporting our communities through philanthropy.
